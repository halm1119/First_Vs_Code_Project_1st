The Philippines is known for warm and friendly people and for having an abundance of beautiful beaches and delicious fruits. Pinoy is an informal demonym referring to the Filipino people in the Philippines and their culture, as well as to overseas Filipinos in the Filipino diaspora. A pinay is a female Filipino.
